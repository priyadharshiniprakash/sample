RECOMMENDED Verify these domains before you proceed
1
Invites can't be undone. Once you send invites, these users can join only by accepting the email. Verifying the domain afterwards won't change that — they'll still need to accept.
2
Each acceptance triggers a second migration pass. When an invited user accepts later, Cliq re-runs mapping to attach their channel history, conversations, and files to the newly-created account. This second pass runs silently in the background — but it adds time to the overall migration and stretches a clean job into hours of trickling completions.
3
Verifying once now saves both. Users on a verified domain are added directly in a single pass — no invite email, no second-pass mapping. Verification typically takes minutes (worst case 48 hours, depending on your DNS provider).
One-click verification supported with GoDaddy Cloudflare IONOS · other providers use a 4-step TXT-record path

Sign in to your DNS provider

2
Add this TXT record
Paste the values below into a new TXT record on your domain's DNS zone.
Host: @  ·  Value: zoho-verification=zb1234567.zmverify.zoho.com
3
Wait for propagation
Most DNS providers propagate within minutes. In rare cases this can take up to 48 hours.
